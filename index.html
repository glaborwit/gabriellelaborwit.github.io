Independent Study: Allergy Learning Game

Children with food allergies are a vulnerable group within our population and could be at risk due to their peers' level of knowledge surrounding allergies. As such, our team created a game for children to learn more about allergies.

OurCS Workshop: Perceptions of Privacy

The rise of privacy and security concerns of digital voice assistants has closely followed their growth in popularity. At the OurCS conference, our group aimed to study the privacy and security perceptions of Amazon Alexa device owners.

Time Warp in AR
