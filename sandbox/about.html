About Me

Hi! My name is Gabrielle LaBorwit and I am in my fourth and final year studying Computer Science and Cognitive Psychology at Bucknell University. As a student with a focus on HCI learning in a sea of future software engineers, I have the unique benefit of understanding the actions and motivations of the programmer as well as the desires of the user. When I am not studying, I enjoy taking photographs, browsing through Medium articles, and cooking.
